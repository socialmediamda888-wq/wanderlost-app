1. Terms & Conditions (The Service Contract)

A. Membership & Subscription

The Model: You are entering into a monthly membership for 20 CHF/month. This is a personal, non-transferable license to use the Wanderløst Elite discovery engine.

Billing: Subscriptions are auto-renewing. You will be billed every 30 days from your signup date.

Cancellation: You may cancel at any time via the "Cancel Membership" button in your Account Settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds are provided for used portions of a month.

B. The Scavenger Hunt & Safety

Sequential Discovery: You acknowledge that the App reveals locations one by one. Wanderløst is not responsible for your inability to reach a location or for the closure of any suggested "Niche Spot."

User Conduct: You agree to respect the "Local Secret" nature of these spots. Sharing revealed locations on public social media (TikTok/Instagram) in a way that leads to overcrowding may result in account suspension to protect the community vibe.

C. AI Disclaimer (EU AI Act 2026 Compliant)

Generative Content: Our "Clue Master" and "Route Finder" are powered by Generative AI. While we strive for accuracy, AI can occasionally "hallucinate" or provide outdated information.

Final Judgment: You are solely responsible for your own safety. Do not follow AI suggestions if they lead to dangerous areas, private property, or violate local laws.

2. Privacy Policy (Data Protection)

Global Standard: GDPR / nFADP / CCPA

A. Data We Collect

Precise Geolocation: We collect your foreground location only when the app is open to unlock the next scavenger hunt stage and create your "Travel Trail."

Behavioral Data: Our AI analyzes which spots you visit and how long you stay to personalize your future trails.

Vector Profiling: We store a mathematical representation of your travel "vibe" (preferences) in our secure vector database.

B. How We Use AI with Your Data

We use an automated AI agent to scan public data and match it with your profile. This processing is necessary to provide the core service of the app.

No Selling: We do not sell your personal movement data to third-party advertisers. Your data stays within the Wanderløst ecosystem.

C. Your Rights

Right to Delete: You can delete your entire account and all associated GPS trails instantly via the "Delete My Data" button in settings.

Data Portability: You may request an export of your "Travel Trails" and "Badges" at any time.
